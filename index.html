The Man Behind the Chair

Eric Dixon

"I'm an award-winning barber, mental health advocate, entrepreneur and artist."

Eric's story begins not with scissors, but with a crisis — a severe scalp condition from unsanitary tools sparked a journey of self-sufficiency, then mastery. After barbering school, years in the industry, and a relentless drive to build something of his own, he founded Kleankut Grooming Lounge, LLC in the DMV area.

But Kleankut is more than a barbershop. Eric has transformed his chair into a space for real conversation — particularly around mental health and the stigma many Black men face in seeking therapy. His openness about his own experience with counseling has changed perspectives, one appointment at a time.

His work has been recognized by Forbes, ABC7, PBS, Radio One, and WJLA, and his clientele experience something rare: genuine craft paired with genuine care.

Questions

Frequently Asked

How do I book an appointment?+
All bookings are done through Vagaro — click any "Book Now" button on this page and you'll be taken directly to our scheduling system. You can choose your service, time, and pay securely online.
Is Kleankut a private barbershop?+
Yes. Kleankut Grooming Lounge is a private, one-on-one experience. You're not waiting in a busy shop — the space and time are reserved exclusively for you.
What areas do you serve?+
Kleankut is located in the DMV area (DC, Maryland, Virginia). Please check the Vagaro booking page for the current location address, as appointments may be required for entry.
Do you offer services for women?+
Yes — our skincare facials are available for both men and women. Eric's expertise in skin health makes our facial services excellent for anyone seeking a premium grooming experience.
What is the cancellation policy?+
We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Last-minute cancellations may incur a fee. Please contact us directly if you have an emergency — we understand life happens.
Can I purchase products without an appointment?+
Yes! Our grooming products are available to purchase through our Vagaro shop online. You can browse and order from the Shop section of this site and have them shipped to you.
How long do appointments typically take?+
Service durations vary. A standard cut is 45 minutes, while the full VIP Package is approximately 2 hours. Exact timing is shown when booking through Vagaro so you can plan accordingly.
Do you offer memberships or recurring packages?+
Yes — monthly memberships are available through Vagaro and offer great value for regular clients. Packages can also be purchased in advance for specific service bundles.
